📋 HOW TO PLAY

You are a police officer driving a patrol car! Your mission: find and arrest all the criminals in the city.

  • 🕹️Move: Left joystick (mobile) or WASD / Arrow keys
  • 👀Look: Right joystick (mobile) or mouse drag
  • 🔍Find criminals: Look for the red markers — drive close to catch them!
  • ⚖️Arrest: Choose to Fine or Send to Jail
  • ⚠️Warning: Don't hit pedestrians!
  • 🌅Day and night will change as you patrol
  • 🏆Complete all 10 levels to win!

Each level has more criminals to catch. Good luck, officer! 🚨
